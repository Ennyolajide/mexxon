ABOUT THE MEXXON PROGRAM

Searching for a job is an exciting but stressful process. You have big hopes and fears and this can make searching for a job more difficult. It can be the most frustrating thing. You spend hours writing what you think is a great CV, send it off with your fingers crossed and then you wait. And wait. For some reason, your application just did not grab the attention of any of the prospective employers out there. This is where the Mexxon Job Search Skills Training Program comes in. Our much sought after Training Program consists of Lectures, Inter-active Sessions and Practical Exercises to enable participants to do mock interviews for example and learn how to do them proficiently with us before they have to go and face a real Job Interview Panel.

LEARNING ENVIRONMENT

The Mexxon Job Search Skills Program is held in our comfortable fully air-conditioned Conference/Training Rooms located in Lekki Phase 1. This course will give you superb CV and Job Application Cover Letter Writing Skills as well as detailed training on all the things you need to know in order to change the current situation you are in from how to quantify your achievements to how to research for job applications to ensure that you secure a “plum” job as quickly as possible. This Course is the only one of its kind to be found anywhere in Nigeria and is unique in that trainees have benefitted from the experience after finding it easier to get good jobs compared to their lack of success before the Program.

This course is designed to give anyone beginning or actively involved in a job search the key skills they need to find the job they are looking for. From students looking for internships to experienced professionals looking to change jobs this will be a valuable skill-set for almost anyone to acquire and apply to their personal, career and professional development. First this important and rare Course will guide you through the different scenarios of people searching for a job. These include post-NYSC graduates as well as people who are looking to return to work after a period of absence, changing career or relocating.
