GENERAL PROGRAM LEARNING OBJECTIVES

You will learn the importance of making a good job choice in your career, identify the difference between a career change and regular job search and learn the strategies to successfully complete a job search. Whether you are about to start job searching after graduation, you have been job searching for some time due to unemployment or you are unhappy with your current job attending this short but critically important course will help position you for success in your job search very quickly.

Next you will receive a clear overview of the Mexxon Ten-Step Job Search Process. This will help you to identify and position yourself for the type of jobs you would like, figure out what employers are looking for, develop very good relationships with prospective employers and stay connected throughout the decision making process. Understanding this process will be a great help for eventually securing your ideal jobs in the future.

LEARNING OBJECTIVES

  • Help you to identify your potential job target(s)
  • Help you to create a powerful job search campaign
  • Help you to prepare and write an outstanding CV
  • How to prepare and write Job Cover Letters
  • Show you how to conduct in-depth jobs research
  • Show you how to network for jobs effectively
  • Teach you how to prepare well for job interviews
  • Help you how to do interviews through mock trials
  • Teach you how to meet the challenge of job searching
  • Teach you how to position yourself for success
  • Teach you how to package yourself & be well presented
  • Show you how to negotiate for a good pay package
